Our studio

A working flower studio on Weber City Road

Karin's Kreations isn't a warehouse pushing pre-boxed bouquets. It's a real local studio — and a small greenhouse and nursery — where arrangements are cut, conditioned and built by hand for the families of Gonzales and the wider Ascension Parish.

Because everything is designed to order, no two pieces are quite the same. Tell us the occasion, the colors and the feeling you're after, and we'll take it from there.
